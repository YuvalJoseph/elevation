FAQ

Question: What is this?

Answer: Just a friendly competition to motivate people to not only ride their bikes in place of driving, but do take the long route, preferably uphill.

Question: Does it cost money to enter?

Answer: Nope. Completely free. You just need a Strava account, which you can get for free at strava.com

Question: What do I get for winning?

Answer: Personal satisfaction and the envy of your peers

Question: How long is the competition?

Answer: This competition is generally held in May (you might have heard it referenced as the "May Elevation Challenge"), but this website will show stats for the current month. So you can keep up the competition as long as you want.
